Features

Everything you need to stay organized

Powerful features crafted for simplicity. Note Vault gives you the tools to capture, organize, and retrieve your ideas effortlessly.

🔐
Secure Storage

Your notes are stored locally with no third-party access. Your thoughts stay completely private, always.

⚡
Instant Search

Find any note in milliseconds with real-time full-text search across titles, content, tags, and categories.

🏷️
Smart Categories

Organize with Work, Personal, Ideas, and Study categories. Filter by priority to focus on what matters.

📦
Archive System

Keep your workspace clean by archiving old notes. Restore anytime — nothing is ever permanently lost.

⭐
Favorites & Pins

Star your most important notes and pin them to the top. Your priorities always within reach.

Dark Mode

Easy on the eyes. Switch between light and dark themes instantly. Your preference is always remembered.

About

Built for real productivity

Note Vault was born from frustration with bloated note apps. We wanted something that felt premium, stayed fast, and got out of the way while you think.

Everything is stored locally — your data never leaves your device. No subscriptions, no tracking, no compromise.

🗂️

Your data, your control

All notes stay in your browser. No servers, no accounts required to get started, no privacy concerns.

  • ✅ Completely free forever
